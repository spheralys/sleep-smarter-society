How it works

Simple enough to follow.
Structured enough to reveal patterns.

Three to five minutes a day. That's it on your side.

01

One daily focus

Each day centers on one sleep-related behavioral factor, something you can understand and apply the same day. No clinical background required.

02

Night notes before bed

A short structured note based on CBT-I sleep journal practice, adapted so you're not pressured to reflect perfectly. Fill it in once, honestly, and move on.

~2 min
03

Morning check-in

A brief observation after waking. How you felt, what you noticed during the night. Nothing more.

~1 min
07

Your behavioral picture emerges

Patterns that were invisible become clear, often changing what you thought the problem was.
